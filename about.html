CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society.
The Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with
leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the twin states of Telangana & AP.
The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of 'useful learning'.
